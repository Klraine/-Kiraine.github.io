How SokoDeals Works

Simple steps to buy and sell items in your community

1. Browse or List

Find great deals or post your items for sale in relevant categories

2. Contact Directly

Call or WhatsApp the seller to discuss price, condition, and viewing

3. Meet & Complete

Arrange safe meeting point, inspect items, and complete the transaction

Safe Trading Tips

Stay safe while buying and selling on SokoDeals

Meet in Public Places

Choose safe, public locations for meetings. Shopping malls, banks, or police stations are recommended.

Inspect Before Buying

Always inspect items thoroughly before payment. Test electronics and check furniture condition.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, walk away. Don't feel pressured to complete any transaction.
